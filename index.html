BOK CHOY

The Flavor Sensation That'll Rock The Nation

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Necessities

  • 10 bulbs of bok choy per person
  • Water
  • Any variety of vegetable oil
  • A decent sized pot
  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Cilantro
  • Stove
  • A Knife
  • Garlic

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Instructions

  1. Fill the pot with water approximately 1/3 the total volume
  2. Turn on the stove and place the pot of water on top of the stove
  3. While the water is boiling wash the bulbs of bok choy
  4. Finely mince approximately 3 stalks cilantro
  5. cut the ginger length-wise so that they resemble chips
  6. Crush your garlic with the flat face of your knife and remove the skin from the rest of the garlic
  7. When the water is brought to a boil add a decent amount of vegetable oil to the water
  8. Then add ginger and garlic immediately afterwards
  9. Place your bok choy into the water and place the lid on top
  10. Wait for 10 minutes
  11. Scoop your bok choy into a bowl along with a bit of the water that it was boiled in
  12. Place the minced cilantro on top
  13. Drizzle some soy sauce of your choice on top
  14. Enjoy!
